🛠️ Daily Maintenance Checklist for ETL-C13090 CO2 Laser
（ETL-C13090 CO2 激光切割机每日保养清单）

To ensure the precision and longevity of your ETL-C13090, please follow this daily routine:
（为确保您的 ETL-C13090 保持高精度和长寿命，请遵循以下日常保养程序：）

1. Lens & Mirrors Cleaning (镜片清洁) 🔍
Check Focal Lens: Dust on the lens can cause overheating and cracking. Use anhydrous ethanol and a lint-free swab.
（检查聚焦镜：镜片上的灰尘会导致过热开裂。请使用无水乙醇和无尘棉签清洁。）

Reflective Mirrors: Ensure the three mirrors are clear of smoke residue to maintain laser power.
（反射镜：确保三面反射镜没有烟尘残留，以保持激光功率稳定。）

2. Water Cooling System (水循环系统) 💧
Water Temp: Ensure the chiller is running and the water temperature is between 18°C - 25°C.
（水温：确保冷水机正常运行，水温保持在 18°C - 25°C 之间。）

Water Flow: Confirm there are no bubbles or kinks in the laser tube's water path.
（水流：确认激光管水路内无气泡或折弯。）

3. Rail & Motion Parts (导轨与传动) ⚙️
Clear Debris: Remove any scrap material or dust from the honeycomb/slat worktable.
（清理残渣：清除蜂窝或刀条工作台上的任何废料或灰尘。）

Lubrication: Wipe the linear rails and apply a thin layer of specialized lubricant if dry.
（润滑：擦拭线性导轨，若干燥请涂抹薄薄一层专用润滑油。）

4. Exhaust System (抽风系统) 🌪️
Fan Check: Ensure the exhaust fan is working correctly to prevent smoke buildup inside the machine.
（风机检查：确保排风扇工作正常，防止机器内部积烟。）
